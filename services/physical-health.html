Physical Health

Research has shown that physical exercise has multiple positive effects on the human body and seniors can benefit greatly from staying physically active. Even moderate exercise can drastically improve the health of those who are growing older and those who may have ailments associated with aging.

Being physically active enables seniors to continue to participate in the activities they love, even as they get older. Self-Sufficient Seniors helps seniors make physical activity a regular part of their life which leads to an increase in independence.
