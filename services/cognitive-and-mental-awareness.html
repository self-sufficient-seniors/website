Cognitive & Mental Awareness

Lack of cognitive health and mental awareness can have profound implications for an individual’s health and well-being. Seniors and others experiencing cognitive decline may be unable to care for themselves or conduct necessary activities of daily living, such as meal preparation and money management.

Cognitive decline can be prevented by taking part in activities and challenges to increase one’s ability. Similar to physical activity, cognitive activity can have a positive impact on lives of seniors.
