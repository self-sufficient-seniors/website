Health & Medical Awareness

For many, simply navigating the health care system can often feel very confusing and frustrating. Especially for seniors staying on top of appointments, medications and test results can quickly become overwhelming.

Having an understanding of one’s personal health care plan, is instrumental in individual’s quality of life. Once knowledgeable of all ailments and diagnoses, one will have a better grasp on their health.

Health Care Advocacy

  • Navigate through the health care system
  • Liaise and communicate between health care practitioners, clients and family
  • Accompany to medical appointments or hospital stays
  • End of life planning
  • File health and medical claims
  • Support and educate on action plan
