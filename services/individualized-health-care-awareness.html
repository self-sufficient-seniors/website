Individualized Health Care Awareness

Navigating the health care system can often be confusing and frustrating for seniors. Having to stay on top of appointments, medications and test results can quickly become overwhelming.

Self-Sufficient Seniors works with clients, in conjunction with their health care professionals, to ensure that they are receiving the attention they deserve.

What we provide:

  • Navigation of the health care system
  • Liaising between client, healthcare practitioners and family
  • Managing of medical appointments and hospital stays
  • End of life planning
  • Filing health and medical claims
