Individualized Health Care Awareness

Navigating the health care system can often be confusing and frustrating for seniors. Having to stay on top of appointments, medications and test results can quickly become overwhelming.

Self-Sufficient Seniors works with clients, in conjunction with all their health care professionals, to ensure that they understand how to keep themselves in good health. If the client is unable to gain this understanding on their own, Self-Sufficient Seniors will liaise with the appropriate care providers to ensure that the client is receiving the attention they deserve. With this support, families can take comfort in knowing that their loved ones are being well represented in all facets of our health care system.

Individualized Health Care Awareness offerings:

  • Navigation of the health care system
  • Liaise between client, healthcare practitioners and family
  • Accompany to medical appointments and hospital stays
  • End of life planning
  • File health and medical claims
