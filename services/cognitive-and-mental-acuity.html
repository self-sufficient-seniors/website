Cognitive & Mental Acuity

Lack of cognitive health and mental awareness can have profound implications for an individual’s health and well-being. Seniors, and others experiencing cognitive decline, may be unable to care for themselves or conduct necessary activities of daily living, such as meal preparation or financial management.

Self-Sufficient Seniors offers a variety of activities geared toward keeping the brain active to help mitigate the risk of a negative shift in cognitive and mental acuity.

Cognitive & Mental Acuity offerings:

  • Reasoning
  • Perception
  • Memory
  • Problem Solving
  • Coordination
  • Neuroplasticity Stimulation
  • Monthly Progress Reports
