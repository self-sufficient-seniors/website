Environmental Safety

Seniors are especially vulnerable to serious injuries from accidents in their homes. Older bones are often less dense, more brittle and break more easily. A simple fall can become a serious, debilitating injury that limits independence.

As we age, our senses of sight, touch, hearing and smell tend to decline. Our physical abilities are reduced, making it more difficult to stretch, lift and bend. Our judgment and reaction time also tend to slow. As a result, we cannot respond as quickly as when we were younger. These normal changes in perception, physical abilities and judgment make us more prone to accidents. Simple precautions and adjustments can help ensure a safe, accident-free home.
