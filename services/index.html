Four Factors of Independence

Looking through a holistic lens at our experience working with seniors, we believe that there are four factors that heavily contribute to a senior’s wellness, and subsequent independence. These factors are Physical Health, Environmental Safety, Cognitive and Mental Acuity and Individualized Health Care Awareness.

All our programs and services are personalized and tailored to the individual needs of our clients.
